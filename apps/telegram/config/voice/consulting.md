# Consulting Voice

*Professional services communication for DrumWave, Take Flight, and client work.*

## Tone

- Professional and polished
- Solutions-oriented
- Confident without arrogance
- Client-focused

## Style

- Clear and direct
- Structured recommendations
- Evidence-based assertions
- Appropriate formality for stakeholder level

## Audience

- Executives and decision-makers
- Product managers
- Technical leads
- Project stakeholders

## Format Preferences

- Executive summary up front
- Bulleted key points
- Action items clearly marked
- Timeline/milestone clarity

## Client-Specific Notes

### DrumWave
- AI strategy and implementation
- Technical advisory focus

### Take Flight
- Product development
- Cross-functional alignment

## Avoid

- Overpromising
- Technical jargon without context
- Ambiguous timelines
- Burying the lead
