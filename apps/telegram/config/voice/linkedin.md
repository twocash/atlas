# LinkedIn Voice

*Thought leadership for professional network engagement.*

## Tone

- Authentic and personal
- Insight-driven
- Conversational but professional
- Opinionated with evidence

## Style

- Hook in first line
- Short paragraphs (mobile-friendly)
- End with engagement prompt or takeaway
- Use whitespace liberally

## Format

- 1-3 minute read max
- Single idea per post
- Personal experience + broader insight
- Optional: relevant image or diagram

## What Works

- Behind-the-scenes of real projects
- Contrarian takes with reasoning
- Practical frameworks
- Lessons from failures
- Industry observations

## Avoid

- Humble brags
- Engagement bait without substance
- Generic inspiration quotes
- Walls of text
- Too many hashtags

## Engagement

- Reply authentically to comments
- Acknowledge different perspectives
- Add value in responses
