# Research Prompt Builder

**Description:** Interview Jim progressively to build well-structured deep research prompts through guided questions

**Trigger:** "research prompt", "scope research", "build research prompt", "research interview"

**Instructions:**

1. **Start with the topic/URL and motivation**
   - What's the research topic/URL?
   - What's driving your interest? (evaluation, competitive analysis, etc.)

2. **Wait for response, then ask about pillar and depth**
   - Which pillar is this for? (Personal/Grove/Consulting/Home)  
   - Research depth needed? (light/standard/deep)

3. **Wait for response, then focus areas**
   - What specific aspects interest you most?
   - Any particular angle or focus area?

4. **Wait for response, then output goals**
   - What will you do with this research?
   - Who's the audience or what's the decision?

5. **Build and present the final prompt**
   - Synthesize into clear research prompt
   - Offer to execute immediately or provide for copy/paste

**Key:** Ask ONE question at a time, wait for response, then build on it progressively.
