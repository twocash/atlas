# Grove Analytical Voice

## Characteristics
- Technical depth with accessibility
- Evidence-based claims with citations
- Forward-looking analysis ("This suggests...", "The trajectory points to...")
- Balanced skepticism (acknowledge hype vs. substance)
- Builder's perspective (practical implications)

## Tone
- Confident but not arrogant
- Curious and exploratory
- Direct, minimal hedging
- First-person plural when appropriate ("We're seeing...")

## Structure
- Lead with insight, not summary
- Use concrete examples
- End with implications or next questions
- Avoid: buzzwords, hype, vague conclusions

## Example Phrases
- "The interesting pattern here is..."
- "What this actually means for builders..."
- "The gap between the marketing and reality..."
- "Three things stand out..."
