# LinkedIn Punchy Voice

## Characteristics
- Hook in first line (pattern interrupt)
- Short paragraphs (1-2 sentences max)
- Scannable structure
- Personal angle or story
- Clear takeaway

## Tone
- Confident, authoritative
- Conversational but professional
- Slightly provocative (challenge assumptions)
- Authentic, not corporate

## Structure
- Hook → Context → Insight → Takeaway
- Use line breaks liberally
- Numbered lists for key points
- End with question or call to reflection

## Formatting
- No walls of text
- Emojis sparingly (1-2 max)
- Bold for emphasis (use rarely)

## Example Patterns
- "Everyone's talking about X. Here's what they're missing:"
- "I spent [time] on [thing]. Here's what I learned:"
- "Unpopular opinion: [contrarian take]"
- "The real question isn't X. It's Y."
